GET STARTED

Our mission is to improve the entire healthcare industry by simplifying the enrollment process, storing relevant patient information securely for all medical providers to access, and decreasing the wait time in hospitals and medical centers.

01. Create a Profile

Fill in your basic personal information through our simple platform. Information may include phone number, home address, and email.

02. Confirm Your identity

Include government identification and healthcare insurance information to confirm your identity.

03. Upload Medical Information

Information will include known allergies, recent visits, and previous and current medication used. This information will be constantly updated in the future as you visit other clinics, hospitals, or doctors.

04. Enter Family Medical History

Medical history will help future medical providers accommodate you in the best possible way.

About Blockchain Technology

Revolutionizing the
healthcare industry, for life.

Blockchain technology is revolutionizing the business of healthcare. At its core, blockchain is a distributed system recording and storing transaction records. More specifically, blockchain is a shared, immutable record of peer-to-peer transactions built from linked transaction blocks and stored in a digital ledger.
